Marine Down

In Oct. 1983, while on a peacekeeping mission to ensure the withdrawal of Yasser Arafat's troops in the region. The marine corps base in Beirut was bombed by suicide bombers driving trucks filled with explosives. Many marines and members of NATO forces were killed in result. You take over as a young marine named Dan "Juke", a survivor of the bombings and his mission for revenge on those that killed his friends. To discover that there are plans to start WWIII. As you venture in the game you will be able to help the people of Lebanon and rebuild your base, which will help in your fight against Arafat.

Save The Boy

In 2100 the world has changed and only a select few can become citizens. Most of them enlist in the military to do it. You joined so your family could become citizens. No one can know about the missions that you have done, but your family is taken care of and to surprise them your return home after your term of service is up on your nephew's birthday of all days. However, your enemies think otherwise, the raid the party shortly after you arrive and kidnap your nephew. Your family is in shock and looks to you to get him back, leaving you with the question, can you Save The Boy?
