Save The Boy

In 2100 the world has changed and only a select few can become citizens. Most of them enlist in the military to do it. You joined so your family could become citizens. No one can know about the missions that you have done, but your family is taken care of and to suprise them your return home after your term of service is up on your nephews birthday of all days. However your enemies think otherwise, the raid the party shortly after you arrive and kidnap your nephew. Your family is in shock ad looks to you to get him back, leaving you with the question, can you Save The Boy?
